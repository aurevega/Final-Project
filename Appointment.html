Appointment

Appointments

We make every effort to reserve enough time for your appointment. Please arrive 10 minutes prior to your appointment as to not inconvenience our other clients. It may be necessary to deduct time from a treatment from late arrivals.

Cancellations

Cancellations must be received at least 24 hours prior to an appointment time. If an appointment is rescheduled or cancelled on the day of service, and the appointment was reserved for three hours or more, you will be charged 50% of what the total cost would have been.

Spa Reservations

All services/appointments are by reservation. We require reservations with a credit card, gift card, or pre-payment that take three hours or more.

Gratuities

Gratuities are at the guests º discretion and appreciated by the staff for outstanding service.

Personal Possessions

We regret that we are not liable for the loss of or damage to personal articles.
